About Us
相关简介

Acorn House After-school tutoring program is designed to provide students with academic support and enrichments outside regular school hours. We are targeting children from kindergarten to eighth grade. Tutoring sessions will be conducted in small groups(6-8) or one-on-one, depending on the students’ needs. Our teachers and tutors are well trained to guide students through academic challenges, help with homework, and extra practice to consolidate their learning skills.
